Welcome to Jaycode.co.uk. This website is operated by Joe Seabrook, a sole trader based in the UK ("I", "me", "my"). I am committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use this website.

2. Data Controller

I am the data controller responsible for your personal data collected via this website. If you have any questions about this policy, you can contact me at:

Email: contact@jaycode.co.uk

3. What Personal Data I Collect

  • Contact Form Data: When you submit a contact form, I collect your name, email address, and message.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and access logs (collected automatically for security purposes but not actively monitored for user tracking).

4. How I Use Your Personal Data

  • To respond to your inquiries submitted via the contact form and to resolve your query.
  • To maintain website security and prevent fraudulent activities.
  • To comply with legal obligations (e.g., responding to lawful requests from authorities).

5. How Your Data is Stored and Processed

  • Contact form submissions are processed via the Gmail API and sent directly to my email inbox.
  • I do not store your personal data in a database or on my servers.
  • My VPS is hosted by IONOS and secured with firewalls, encryption, and other security measures.
  • SSL encryption is used to secure data transmissions.

6. Sharing of Your Data

I do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. However, I may disclose personal data when required by law, legal process, or governmental request.

7. Your Data Protection Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of the personal data I hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You can request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal requirements.
  • Right to Restriction of Processing: You can request limitations on how I use your data.
  • Right to Object: You can object to data processing in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request transfer of your data to another service provider.

To exercise these rights, contact me at contact@jaycode.co.uk.

8. Data Retention

I only retain personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Contact form messages are stored only for the time required to respond to and resolve your inquiry, after which they are deleted within 90 days unless required for legal or business record-keeping purposes.

9. Security Measures

  • Firewalls (UFW) and intrusion prevention systems (Fail2Ban) on my VPS.
  • SSL encryption for secure data transmission.
  • Regular security updates and system monitoring.
